• In general, six broad sources of data/information to help construct reasonable prior distributions:
1) Physical/chemical theories

3) Previous (historical) data/evidence of the current version of the product design and/or process

• Manufacturing/Assembly Phase: On-line production data, HASS, Burn-In, ESS
• Field Deployment Phase: data from real world customer use, annual qualification testing
4) Supplier data
• Look for surrogate data with correlation to the interested characteristics; sub-system test data
5) Competitive analysis
• Component level analysis with industry-wide available data and/or supplier data
• System and/or product level testing (e.g., competitive vehicle testing to 100K miles)
6) Expert judgements (subjective in nature; through survey and analysis)
